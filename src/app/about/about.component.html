About Us

Sweety is a renowned sweets shop located in the heart of Cairo. Established over a decade ago, we have been committed to providing our customers with the finest quality sweets made from the best ingredients.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring joy to our customers through our delicious and unique sweet treats. We strive to maintain the highest standards of quality and service to ensure that every visit to Sweety is a delightful experience.

Our Products

We offer a wide range of sweets, including traditional Egyptian desserts and modern confectioneries. Some of our popular products include:

  • Basbousa
  • Kunafa
  • Baklava
  • Chocolate Truffles
  • Macarons
